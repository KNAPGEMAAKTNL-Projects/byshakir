### How Long Does a Typical Project Take?

A single-room redesign typically takes 8 to 12 weeks. A complete home interior, from concept through installation, runs 6 to 12 months. International projects can take longer due to shipping and local building regulations. We agree a realistic timeline at the start of every commission. For a more detailed overview, see [our design process](/services/).

> 55 words · References service page for full process detail.
> Specific numbers: 8–12 weeks, 6–12 months.

### What Is the Difference Between Interior Design and Interior Decorating?

Interior design covers the full scope of a residential project: spatial planning, material specification, bespoke joinery, procurement and trade coordination. Interior decorating focuses on surface finishes, furnishings and styling. By Shakir practises interior design and [interior architecture](/services/), meaning we work with spatial structure, not just aesthetics.
