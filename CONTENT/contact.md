## Intro (20–40 words)

Tell us about your project. We read every enquiry personally and respond within 24 hours with a suggestion for a first meeting, either in person or by video call.

> 30 words · Specific about what "contact" triggers:
> 1. What happens: we read it personally
> 2. When: within 24 hours
> 3. What the response contains: a suggestion for a meeting
> 4. Format options: in person or video call
>
> Distinguishes response time (24h) from offerte/quote time (not promised).
