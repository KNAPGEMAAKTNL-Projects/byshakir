## Description

### What Is Included in a Complete Interior Project?

A By Shakir commission covers everything between your first conversation and the moment you walk into a finished home. Spatial planning, material selection, furniture specification, bespoke joinery, procurement of every item and coordination of all trades through to installation and final dressing.

The scope is deliberately broad. Most clients come to us because they do not want to manage ten different suppliers, coordinate deliveries or second-guess material choices alone. We take on that complexity so you can focus on the decisions that matter: the feeling you want, the way you live, the pieces you love.

For clients who want to focus on a single element, individual services such as [3D visualisation](/services/3d-design/) or furniture procurement are also available. The scope is shaped around what your project needs, not a fixed package.

This service is designed for homeowners planning a complete renovation, new build or significant refurbishment. Projects typically begin at a scale where professional coordination genuinely saves time and cost: full-home interiors, multi-room renovations, or international properties where managing suppliers remotely would be impractical.

## Process

### How Does the Design Process Work?

Every project follows a clear sequence, though the pace is set by you.

**1. Conversation.** Not a sales meeting. A genuine exchange about how you live, what your home needs to do and the feeling you want it to hold.

**2. Concept development.** From the conversation, a design direction takes shape: spatial layouts, a material palette and early visualisation. We present options; you choose a direction.

**3. 3D visualisation.** We produce [photorealistic renders](/services/3d-design/) of every room. You see your home before construction begins. This is where changes are easiest and cost nothing.

**4. Procurement and build.** With the design approved, we source and order all materials, furniture, fixtures and bespoke joinery. We coordinate every trade, manage every timeline and oversee the build.

**5. Dressing and handover.** The final stage: placing every cushion, every artwork, every object. You walk into a finished home.

## FAQ

### How Long Does a Typical Interior Design Project Take?

It depends on scope. A single-room redesign might take 8 to 12 weeks. A complete home interior, from concept through installation, typically runs 6 to 12 months. International projects can take longer due to shipping and local regulations. We set a realistic timeline together at the start and update you at every stage.

> 53 words · Opens with direct answer.

### Do I Need to Be Present During the Design Process?

Not for most of it. Many clients are based in one country while their property is in another. The [3D visualisation stage](/services/3d-design/) is specifically designed for remote approval: you see every room in photorealistic detail and sign off digitally. We handle all site coordination and send regular photo updates during installation.
