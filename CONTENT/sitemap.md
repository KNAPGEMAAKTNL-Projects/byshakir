## SEO Strategy Overview

### Market Position
By Shakir occupies a niche at the intersection of Dutch-based luxury interior design and international project scope. The competitive landscape (COMPETITORS.md) reveals:
- **Local competitors** (Set Interieur, Laura Hindriks, Su Casa) have weak SEO and no international presence
- **National leaders** (Piet Boon, Kolenik, Lagrand) dominate branded terms but leave long-tail international queries underserved
- **By Shakir's edge**: international project locations (Dubai, Barcelona, Malaga, Antwerp) create keyword opportunities no regional competitor can match

### Keyword Philosophy
- **No keyword stuffing** — natural integration only. Google's December 2025 core update penalises obvious manipulation
- **Entity-rich copy** — specific project names, locations, materials, processes. AI Overviews cite pages with 20.6% proper noun density
- **Question-format H2s** — 60–70% of H2 headings phrased as questions (7x citation impact for smaller domains, SE Ranking 2025)
- **Answer capsules** — 40–60 word direct-answer paragraphs under each H2, optimised for AI Overview and featured snippet extraction

### AI SEO Approach
Pages with FAQ content are 2.7–3.2x more likely to appear in AI Overviews. Each core page includes an AI-targetable question it can answer authoritatively. The international scope and specific project case studies create entity-dense, citeable content that generic competitor pages lack.
