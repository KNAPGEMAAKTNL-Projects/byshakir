## Services

Three named service tiers on the site:

### 1. Complete Luxury Interiors
Full-room interior design including floors, walls, furniture, and accessories. Shakir coordinates all elements to create a unified result.

### 2. Customized Solutions
Bespoke furniture design — custom-made upholstered pieces in client-selected fabrics and color combinations. Emphasis on exclusive fabrics sourced by Shakir.

### 3. Bespoke Collections
Exclusive upholstered furniture pieces; production appears to be partly in-house or through controlled suppliers.

Before execution, 3D drawings and sketches are made from floor plans. Clients receive visual impressions of proposed furniture and accessories before committing.

6. **Portfolio** — are all 5 projects still active/completed? Any new projects to add?
7. **Phone number** — which number should be the primary contact: 0344-655473 or 06-18658059?
8. **Showroom** — is the Tiel showroom still the only physical location?
9. **International focus** — Dubai, Barcelona, Malaga, Antwerp. Accurate? Any new locations?
10. **Fragrances / accessories product line** — is this still active? Was it ever a separate offering?
